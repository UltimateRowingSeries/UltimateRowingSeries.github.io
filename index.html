Ultimate Rowing Series

A Munster-based rowing series for teams of five: four rowers and one cox, competing across fixed seat, flatwater, coastal, and beach sprint disciplines.

View Events View Leaderboard

One team. Four disciplines. Ultimate crew.

The Ultimate Rowing Series brings together crews from across Munster to test all-round rowing ability. Each team is made up of four rowers and a cox, with points earned across a range of regatta formats.

The Disciplines

Fixed Seat

Traditional team racing built on timing, power, and endurance.

Flatwater

Classic regatta racing on calm water, rewarding speed and precision.

Coastal

Open water racing where crews must handle changing sea conditions.

Beach Sprint

Fast, explosive racing from beach to boat and back again.
